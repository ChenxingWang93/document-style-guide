n = 1

An nth-degree Bezier curve is defined by n-阶贝塞尔曲线 由定义

C(u) = \sum_{i=0}^{n}B_{i,n}(u)P_{i}, 0<=u<=1,

Figure 1.8. Cubic curves. 3次 曲线 (a) Three-dimensional twisted; 3维 扭曲

(b) Inflection point; 拐点

(c) Cusp; 尖点

(d) Loop. 循环

the geometric coefficients of this form, are called control points. 式中的几何系数

P_{i}

are called control points 称为 控制点

Notice that the definition, Eq.(1.7), requires that 定义 要求

u \in [0,1]
B_{i,n}(u) = \frac{n!}{i!(n-i)!}n!(1-u)^{n-i}

Eq.(1.8)

B_{0,1}(u) = 1 - u
B_{1,1}(u) = u
C(u) = \sum_{i=0}^{n}B_{i,n}(u)P_{i} 0 <= u <= 1

Eq.(1.7)

takes the form 取形式

C(u) = (1 - u)P_{0}+uP_{1}

a straight line segment from 一个直线片段

P_{0}

to

P_{1}
